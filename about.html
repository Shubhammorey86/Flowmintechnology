Who We Are

Flowmin Technology is a fintech infrastructure and solution provider that helps businesses launch, scale, and operate payment products without building everything from scratch. We serve payment gateway companies, fintech startups, NBFCs, reseller networks, and software platforms that need reliable payment infrastructure they can depend on.

Based in India and serving clients globally, we provide the technical backbone for payment gateways, AEPS services, white-label solutions, and API integrations. Our focus is simple: give businesses the tools and support they need to move fast, stay compliant, and build trust with their customers.

Our Approach

Reliability & Uptime

We understand that downtime in payments means lost revenue and broken customer trust. Our infrastructure is built for high availability, with redundancy and monitoring to ensure your services stay online when your customers need them most.

Compliance-First Thinking

Payments is a regulated industry, and compliance is non-negotiable. We stay ahead of RBI guidelines, NPCI requirements, and data security standards so you don't have to worry about regulatory changes disrupting your business.

Engineering + Support Mindset

Good technology isn't enough. We combine strong engineering with responsive support, so you get both a product that works and a team that helps when you need it. Technical documentation, sandbox environments, and real people who understand your questions.

Transparent Communication

No surprises, no hidden clauses. We believe in clear pricing, honest timelines, and open communication about what we can and cannot do. If something goes wrong, we tell you immediately and work to fix it. If a feature isn't ready, we're upfront about it.
